# 📊 Amazon Sales & Operations Analytics (India)

## 🔍 Overview

This project analyzes over **128,000+ real-world e-commerce transactions** to uncover insights into sales performance, operational efficiency, and customer order behavior. The goal is to simulate a real business scenario and provide data-driven recommendations to improve performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and order trends
* Evaluate order status (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned)
* Identify operational inefficiencies and revenue leakage
* Assess fulfillment performance across methods
* Discover top-performing products and categories

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **Excel** – Data cleaning, KPI calculations, pivot analysis
* **SQL** – Data querying, aggregations, performance analysis
* **Python (Pandas)** – Data cleaning and exploratory analysis
* **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard and data visualization

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

* Total Sales
* Total Orders
* Cancellation Rate (%)
* Delivered Orders (%)
* Monthly Sales Trends
* Category-wise Performance

---

## 📈 Dashboard Overview

### 🔥 Page 1: Business Overview

* Total Sales, Total Orders, Cancellation Rate
* Monthly Sales Trend

### 🔥 Page 2: Operations Analysis

* Order Status Breakdown
* Fulfillment Performance

### 🔥 Page 3: Product Insights

* Sales by Category
* Top Performing Products

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* A significant portion of revenue is generated by a few top-performing categories
* Noticeable cancellation rates indicate potential operational inefficiencies
* Certain fulfillment methods show higher delivery success rates
* A small percentage of products contribute to the majority of total revenue (Pareto principle)

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Optimize underperforming fulfillment channels to reduce cancellations
* Focus marketing and inventory on high-performing categories
* Investigate reasons for order cancellations and improve order handling
* Leverage top-performing products for revenue growth strategies

---
